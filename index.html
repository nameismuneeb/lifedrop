What Is Blood Donation?

Blood donation is the voluntary process of giving blood to help people who need it for medical treatment. Donated blood is used during surgeries, accidents, childbirth, and for patients suffering from serious illnesses. A healthy person can safely donate blood, and a single donation can save multiple lives. Blood cannot be artificially produced, so donations from volunteers are essential for hospitals and emergency care.
